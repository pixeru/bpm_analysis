How to Interpret Results

S1 and S2 Heart Sounds

S1 (lub): First heart sound, marked in blue, occurs when the tricuspid and mitral valves close.
S2 (dub): Second heart sound, marked in red, occurs when the aortic and pulmonary valves close.

BPM Interpretation

Normal: 60-100 BPM at rest
Bradycardia: Below 60 BPM
Tachycardia: Above 100 BPM
